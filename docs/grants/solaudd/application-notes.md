# ArcPay x AUDD Grant Notes

## Project Details

ArcPay is building treasury infrastructure that can help the AUDD ecosystem grow by making stablecoin operations usable for AI-agent businesses and internet-native teams.

The problem is that global operators often need programmable treasury workflows, but most onchain payment stacks still stop at simple transfers. Teams need a way to receive funds, manage invoices, control contractor payouts, apply treasury policy, route capital, and keep auditable records, all while using stable settlement assets that match real business needs.

ArcPay solves this by turning stablecoins into business operations. Inside ArcPay, users can onboard with wallet-first identity, create payment requests, manage invoices, track contractors, enforce policies, run counterparty checks, and prepare treasury actions from one control surface. For AUDD specifically, the opportunity is to make AUD-denominated stable settlement part of a real operating workflow rather than just another token in a wallet.

This would help grow the AUDD ecosystem by giving founders, AI-agent teams, and cross-border operators a practical way to use AUDD for treasury management, contractor payouts, invoice collection, and stable-value business operations on Solana.

## Grant Amount Justification

I’m applying for 5000 AUDD to complete and harden AUDD as a first-class treasury rail inside ArcPay.

The grant would be used to fund the remaining productization work required to turn the current MVP into a more production-ready stablecoin operating flow: deeper AUDD support across payments and invoices, stronger mainnet reliability, policy-aware payout and treasury controls, backend infrastructure, testing, deployment costs, and user-facing documentation.

This amount would let me move beyond a hackathon prototype and ship a more complete AUDD-powered treasury experience for AI-agent businesses, cross-border operators, and teams that want AUD-denominated stable settlement on Solana.

The grant will be used to complete and harden AUDD as a first-class treasury rail inside ArcPay.

Allocation plan:

The goal is to use the grant to move ArcPay from a hackathon MVP into a more production-ready treasury product that can drive practical AUDD usage for AI-agent businesses and cross-border operators on Solana.

## Goals and Milestones

1. Complete deeper AUDD support across ArcPay’s payment requests, invoices, and treasury workflows.
2. Improve mainnet reliability and production readiness for stablecoin operations, including wallet flows, routing, and policy enforcement.
3. Make AUDD usable as part of a real operator workflow for AI-agent businesses, including invoicing, payout preparation, and treasury management.
4. Publish user-facing documentation and demo flows showing how AUDD can be used inside a private treasury operating system on Solana.
5. Validate the product with real usage by onboarding early users and testing AUDD-denominated business flows end to end.

## Primary KPI

The primary KPI will be the number of successful AUDD-based treasury workflows completed inside ArcPay on mainnet.

Success means demonstrating real end-to-end usage across at least one or more of the following: payment requests, invoice flows, contractor payout preparation, or treasury operations using AUDD as the operating asset.

A strong success outcome would be:
- at least 10 successful AUDD-denominated workflow completions
- at least 3 active test users or teams using the product during the project period
- stable production performance with no critical blocking issues in the main AUDD flow
